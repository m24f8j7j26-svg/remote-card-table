How the process works

  1. One player clicks Host room and shares the room code by text, phone, or video chat.
  2. The other player opens this same page, enters the code, and clicks Join.
  3. South and North draft from the deck two cards at a time until each player has 13 cards.
  4. On your draft turn, keep the first card and discard the next, or discard the first and keep the next.
  5. Bid, play legal cards, and keep talking while the shared table updates for both players.
  6. Extra tricks are bags. Every 5 bags costs 50 points, with leftover bags carrying forward.
